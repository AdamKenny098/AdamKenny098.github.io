Echoes of the Labyrinth

Descend Into the Unknown

Echoes of the Labyrinth is a procedural dungeon crawler where every descent is different. Navigate shifting corridors, face intelligent enemies, and uncover the secrets buried deep within an ever-changing maze.

Features

  • Procedurally generated dungeon floors
  • AI-driven enemies using Behaviour Trees
  • Dynamic loot, quests, and progression systems
  • First-person combat with class-based abilities
  • Expandable systems for future content and scaling

Technical Highlights

  • Dungeon generation using BSP, Delaunay triangulation, and MST path selection
  • Modular system architecture for quests, loot, AI, UI, and combat
  • NavMesh-based AI with sight, hearing, and state-driven behaviour
  • Procedural room decoration, enemy spawning, and dungeon progression
