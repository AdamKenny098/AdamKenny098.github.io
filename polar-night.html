Polar Night

A Frozen Nightmare

Trapped in the frozen arctic, you must scavenge, survive, and outlast a dying cold. Polar Night is a single-player survival experience inspired by the harshness of real Arctic research stations. Avoid hypothermia, ration fuel and food, and escape the blizzard.

Features

  • Dynamic day-night cycle and generator system
  • Fuel management and freezing mechanics
  • Explorable zones, equipment upgrades, and events
